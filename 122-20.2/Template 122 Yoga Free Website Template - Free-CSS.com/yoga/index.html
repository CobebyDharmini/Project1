Rituals

These are the sacred rituals of a traditional Indian wedding — from Kanyadaanam to Saptapadi, each step is pure and timeless.

Kanyadaanam

Kanyadaanam — a heartfelt ritual where the bride’s parents lovingly offer their daughter to the groom, entrusting her to a new life. .

Jeelakarra Bellam

Jeelakarra Bellam — a sacred ritual symbolizing the sweet unity of hearts and souls at the start of the wedding.

Thalli Katadam

Thali Katadam — the sacred moment when the groom ties the mangalsutra (thali) around the bride’s neck, symbolizing their eternal bond.

Saptapadi

Saptapadi — the seven sacred steps taken by the couple around the holy fire, each step signifying a vow for their shared journey ahead.
